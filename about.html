Our Story

Who We Are

Thai Terra Gardens is a Thai-based plant sourcing and export company founded by Mr. Monai Suriya, who has over 11 years of experience in the industry. During this time, we have sourced and shipped a wide range of plants from the finest nurseries in Thailand to the Middle East and other countries worldwide.

Our company is dedicated to providing quality choices for individuals looking for plants and garden supplies, as well as serving contractors who need a reliable sourcing partner. With deep knowledge of both Thai flora and international market needs, we bridge Thailand's finest nurseries with buyers around the world.
