Mission & Vision

Our guiding principles and commitment to excellence in plant export

Our Purpose

What Drives Us

We were founded on the belief that growing Thai and tropical plants in your garden is not difficult anymore. Our goal is to change the way you think about importing plants.

🎯

Our Mission

To deliver excellent-condition plants at the lowest price within the timeframe our customers set — from planting in Thailand to receiving at destination.

🌏

Our Vision

To be the most trusted plant exporter in Thailand, connecting the world with the beauty of Thai tropical flora while supporting local nurseries and communities.

⭐

Our Promise

Every offer and every client deserves special care. No matter how small or large your order, we personalize our service and guarantee delivery within the discussed timeframe.

Core Values

What We Stand For

  • 🏆

    Quality Excellence

    Maintaining and supervising numerous nurseries to ensure healthy propagation and achieve instant response to customer needs at the lowest price.

  • 🤝

    Ownership

    We take full responsibility for every order — from the moment a plant is selected in our nursery to the moment it arrives safely at your destination.

  • 💰

    Fair Trade

    We believe in transparent pricing. High quality products at reasonable prices — no hidden costs, no surprises.

  • 🌱

    Sustainability

    By investing in local nurseries and working with local businesses, we support sustainable agriculture and the Thai economy.

  • 📞

    Responsiveness

    We are always available at your service. Quick responses, proactive communication, and full transparency throughout the process.

  • 📦

    Safe Delivery

    Through specific handling, storage, and packaging policies, we assure the best quality for clients with minimum damage upon arrival.

Thai Terra Gardens

A Thai company for exporting plants with more than 11 years of experience. Established nurseries and local partnerships make us the best plant exporter in Thailand.
